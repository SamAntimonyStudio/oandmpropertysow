2.The Engagement

O&M Property is launching as an independent agency across the Mornington Peninsula, stepping out from under a franchise. Antimony Studio is engaged to build the brand and take it to market, then run six months of channel management and growth, as a single 12-month partnership.

The engagement is delivered in two phases. Phase One is the launch: the brand, identity, website, collateral and channel setup that puts O&M Property live. Phase Two is the growth program: six months of channel management, content, search, lead generation and reporting that turns the new brand into enquiries, appraisals and managements across both the sales team and the rent roll.

This document sets out the scope, timeline, investment and terms of that engagement. It becomes binding on signature by both parties and receipt of the first monthly payment.

Scope of Work

3.Scope of Work

Phase One — The Launch $56,000 + GST

The complete brand and launch system, delivered ahead of the 7 November launch.

  • Strategy: competitor analysis, positioning, tone of voice and content strategy.
  • Brand identity: logo, lockup, insignia, colour, typography, full design system and brand guidelines.
  • Website: custom design and development, integrated with the tech stack, CRM and channels, with listings feed, agent profiles and enquiry capture.
  • Print, collateral & signage: stationery, welcome pack, brochures, for-sale boards and rental signage, print-ready for suppliers. Size and format variants as specified, not print quantities.
  • Channel setup: channel design, introduction and link building, plus content templates for Instagram, LinkedIn, Facebook and Google.

Phase Two — The Growth Program · 6 months $22,000 + GST

Six months of channel management and growth, built for an agency running both a rent roll and a sales team.

  • Channel management: ongoing, on-brand posting and community management across Instagram, LinkedIn, Meta and Google.
  • Content engine: community content covering local businesses, events, schools and area stories.
  • Search & visibility: local SEO, Google Business Profile and high-intent paid search management.
  • Lead generation & lifecycle: website capture, CRM automation, SMS and email nurture across buyers, vendors and the rent roll.
  • Founder & agent brand: founder profile support and the foundation for agent sub-brands.
  • Reporting & optimisation: monthly data-driven reporting tied to rent roll and sales performance.

Optional — Office Signage $3,700 + GST

Not included unless elected in writing. Front desk, frontage and portable bifold sign, design and print-ready artwork only. Production and delivery are not included and are quoted separately by the signage supplier.

Terms & Conditions

7.Terms & Conditions

7.1 Commencement & term

This agreement commences on the effective date and continues for twelve (12) months from the kickoff workshop, unless terminated in accordance with clause 7.8.

7.2 Fees & GST

All fees are stated exclusive of GST unless marked otherwise, and GST is payable in addition. Invoices are issued monthly in advance and are payable within seven (7) days of the invoice date. Overdue amounts may accrue interest at 2% per month and the Studio may suspend work on any overdue account until it is brought current.

7.3 Intellectual property

All concepts, designs, files and materials created by the Studio remain the intellectual property of the Studio until the fees to which they relate are paid in full. On full payment of the relevant fees, ownership of the final, approved deliverables transfers to the Client for use in its business. The Studio retains ownership of preliminary concepts, working files, source files and its own tools, methods and know-how, and retains the right to feature the work in its portfolio and marketing.

7.4 Client responsibilities

The Client will provide timely feedback, approvals, brand assets and information, nominate a single decision-maker for sign-off, and respond to key decisions or blockers within a reasonable period, targeting 24 hours. Delays in feedback, approvals or the provision of materials may move the timeline and are not the responsibility of the Studio.

7.5 Revisions

Each major deliverable includes two (2) rounds of revisions. Further rounds, or changes to scope or direction after approval, are billed separately at the Studio's standard rates and quoted before work proceeds.

7.6 Third-party costs & media spend

Third-party costs, including printing, production, fabrication and installation of signage, fonts, stock imagery, subscriptions, subcontractors and all advertising and media spend, are separate from the fees in this agreement and are funded by the Client. Advertising and media budgets are set and approved by the Client and are not included in the growth program fee.

7.7 Confidentiality

Each party will keep the other's confidential information private and use it only for the purposes of this engagement, both during the term and after it ends.

7.8 Termination & Phase One protection

Either party may terminate this agreement by giving thirty (30) days' written notice. All fees for work performed and committed up to the agreed departure date remain payable.

Phase One — the Launch — carries a fee of $56,000 + GST and is delivered ahead of full payment under the ramped monthly schedule. The full Phase One fee is earned in full on delivery of Phase One. If either party terminates this agreement after Phase One has been delivered, the entire unpaid balance of the $56,000 + GST Phase One fee becomes immediately due and payable in full within thirty (30) days of the agreed departure date, together with the fee for any growth program work committed or performed up to that date. This clause protects the Studio's investment in the launch, which is built and delivered before it is fully paid, and survives termination of this agreement.

7.9 Liability

To the extent permitted by law, the Studio's total liability under this agreement is limited to the fees paid by the Client for the work giving rise to the claim. Neither party is liable for indirect or consequential loss, including loss of profit, revenue or opportunity. Nothing in this agreement limits any rights the Client has under the Australian Consumer Law that cannot lawfully be excluded.

7.10 Force majeure

Neither party is liable for delay or failure to perform caused by events beyond its reasonable control. Affected timelines are extended by a reasonable period.

7.11 Governing law

This agreement is governed by the laws of Victoria, Australia, and the parties submit to the non-exclusive jurisdiction of its courts.

7.12 Entire agreement

This document is the entire agreement between the parties on its subject matter and replaces any prior proposals or discussions. Any variation must be agreed in writing by both parties.
